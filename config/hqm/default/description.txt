Welcome to BlackStoneBlock.
This modpack is focused on the Create mod and it's inspired by the Stoneblock modpack.
This pack is an Expert pack with lots of custom recipes and custom textures.
There's three pages on this quest book.
The aim is to show how to move forward at the start of your adventure, as well as the overall progress of the pack, in this blackstone cave.
The goals page is here to give you some goals to accomplish.

Have fun and good luck !
